Ausgelegt is a layout generator for iOS. Select a format, configure elements, export a print-ready layout. No design skills required.

The app was built in a twelve-week university project. Starting from the iOS Human Interface Guidelines, we developed our own component library. Three rounds of iteration refined the flows and screens. It was my first real encounter with systematic interface thinking. Guidelines as a foundation, not a reference.
